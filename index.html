About Netrakona

Netrakona is a beautiful district in the Mymensingh Division of Bangladesh. Known for its natural beauty, rivers, and peaceful environment, Netrakona is a place of both history and culture. The people here are warm and friendly, and the lifestyle is simple and traditional.

Culture

Netrakona has a rich cultural tradition, with local music, Baul songs, and folk festivals. The region celebrates festivals like Pohela Boishakh and Nabanna with great joy. Many local artisans and musicians have contributed to Bangladeshi culture.

Local Food

The cuisine in Netrakona includes traditional Bengali dishes such as panta ilish, shorshe ilish, rice with lentils, and seasonal sweets like pitha. Freshwater fish is also very popular due to the many rivers in the region.
